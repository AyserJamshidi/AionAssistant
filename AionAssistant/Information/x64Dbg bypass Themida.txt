1) Download x64dbg
2) Download Themidie -- https://github.com/VenTaz/Themidie
3) Download ScyllaHide -- https://github.com/x64dbg/ScyllaHide
4) Import both into x64dbg
5) Disable every ScyllaHide option except for "Kill Anti-Attach"
6) Follow instructions for making two Aion executables and necessary REGEDIT modifications here -- https://stackoverflow.com/questions/40406225/how-to-add-parameters-to-a-running-process-or-exe-by-default/72427580#72427580
7) Use Themidie plugin to start Aion.exe
